History Of Nail Arts

Nail art started as a symbol of status, power, and identity in ancient cultures like China, Egypt, and Babylonia. Even before it became "fashionable," decorated nails had deep cultural meaning.

  • In many Indigenous, African, and Asian cultures, nail decoration was a form of expression.
  • Instagram (2010s) allowed artists to post detailed shots of their designs and tag products or salons.
  • YouTube tutorials taught people how to do nail art at home.
  • Pinterest & TikTok helped users find inspiration and show off their own creations.
